EASY CUBE



First you need to know how to solve a rubik cube.




1. Checkerboard -

This is the most common pattern and this makes every side of cube looks like X

Formula - U2 D2 F2 B2 L2 R2




2. in a cube pattern -


Looks like a cube is in a cube!

Formula - F L F U’ R U F2 L2 U’ L’ B D’ B’ L2 U




3. Plus minus



Except 2 sides, every side has + and - symbol

Formula - U2 R2 L2 U2 R2 L2




4. Four Centers Swap-



Swaps 4 center pieces

Formula - F2 B2 U D’ R2 L2 U D’




5. Green Mamba -



Lines on all sides

Formula - R D R F R' F’ B D R’ U’ B’ U D2
